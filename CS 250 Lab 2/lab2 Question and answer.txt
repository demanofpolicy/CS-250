Questions and Answers

1. What are the four levels of exception safety? No-throw guarantee, String Exception safety, Basic Exception safety
and No Exception safety
2. How many catch blocks can there be with a single try block? one.
3. Which exceptions are children of the logic error exception? Invalid argument, Out of range and length error
4. Which exceptions are children of the runtime error exception? Overflow_error, Underflow error and Range error.
